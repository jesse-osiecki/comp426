Project description

Concept

We want to make it easier to sell your stuff online. We propose to create site that will empower clients to make informed choices about where to sell used electronic equipment, weighing criteria such as sale price as well ease and simplicity.

The homepage of our site will primarily revolve around a central search box, where the user will enter the product to sell (step 1). The search box will be auto populated with suggestions (to match existing list of specifications).

Once a product is selected, a new page will be generated where few further details will be requested (step 2). This information will be used to generate the next page of recommedations of websites or ways to sell your stuff (step 3). This will list sites such as Amazon Used, Ebay, Chegg, Craigslist. The user will be provided an estimate of the market value of his/her item for sale, an ease of use grade, as well as a Sell here button. The sell here button will then redirect (step 4) to the user to the appropriate website (in new tab) to sell his/her item.
